# Sequence Structure Template

## Overview
- **Sequence Length**: 14 days
- **Total Touchpoints**: 8
- **Channels**: Email, LinkedIn, Phone

## Sequence Steps

### Day 1: Initial Email
- **Channel**: Email
- **Purpose**: Introduce relevant insight, spark curiosity
- **Tone**: Professional, research-driven

### Day 2: LinkedIn Connection
- **Channel**: LinkedIn
- **Purpose**: Establish social presence, personalized connection
- **Tone**: Casual, peer-to-peer

### Day 4: Follow-up Email
- **Channel**: Email
- **Purpose**: Share additional value, different angle
- **Tone**: Helpful, consultative

### Day 5: LinkedIn Message
- **Channel**: LinkedIn
- **Purpose**: Continue conversation if connected
- **Tone**: Conversational, curious

### Day 7: Phone + Voicemail
- **Channel**: Phone
- **Purpose**: Add human element, break through noise
- **Tone**: Energetic, concise

### Day 9: Value-Add Email
- **Channel**: Email
- **Purpose**: Share relevant content/insight
- **Tone**: Giving, no ask

### Day 11: Final Email
- **Channel**: Email
- **Purpose**: Create gentle urgency, clear CTA
- **Tone**: Direct but respectful

### Day 14: Breakup Email
- **Channel**: Email
- **Purpose**: Last attempt, permission to close loop
- **Tone**: Understanding, door open

## Outreach.io CSV Format

When exporting, use these columns:
- `email` - Prospect email address
- `first_name` - Prospect first name
- `last_name` - Prospect last name
- `title` - Job title
- `company` - Company name
- `sequence_name` - Name of the sequence
- `step_number` - Step in sequence (1-8)
- `step_type` - Email/LinkedIn/Phone
- `step_day` - Day number
- `subject` - Email subject (if applicable)
- `body` - Message content
